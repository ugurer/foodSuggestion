## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I use the app?
1. Open the app and select your current mood
2. Get personalized food suggestions based on your mood
3. Find nearby restaurants that serve your suggested dish

### Why does the app need my location?
We use your location solely to find restaurants near you. Your location data is not stored on our servers.

### How do I save my favorite restaurants?
Tap the heart icon on any restaurant card to save it to your favorites.

### The app is not working properly
Try the following:
1. Close and reopen the app
2. Check your internet connection
3. Update to the latest version from the App Store

### How do I change my dietary preferences?
Go to Settings tab and toggle your dietary preferences (Vegan, Vegetarian, Gluten-Free).
